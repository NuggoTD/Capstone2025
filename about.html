Meet Andrew - Founder of AH Construction

With a lifetime of experience in the construction industry, Andrew has dedicated his career to mastering the craft of building and renovation. Over the years, he honed his skills by working on a variety of projects, learning from seasoned professionals, and developing a deep understanding of quality craftsmanship.

Driven by the desire for greater independance and a commitment to delivering high-quality results, Andrew took the lap to establish AH Construction. Getting his general contractor's license wasn't just a career move--it was a step toward his dream of leading projects his way, ensuring every build meets the highest standards of excellence.

"I love taking a client’s vision and turning it into reality. Seeing a homeowner's excitement when a project is completed is what makes this work so rewarding." - Andrew Heidelberger
